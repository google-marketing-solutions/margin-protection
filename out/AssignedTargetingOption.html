Class: AssignedTargetingOption

AssignedTargetingOption(id, targetingType, inheritance, targetingDetailsKey, targetingDetails)

An extension of `DisplayVideoResource` to represent an assigned targeting option. It is either assigned to an `Advertiser`, `Campaign`, `InsertionOrder` or `LineItem`.
